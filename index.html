Welcome to the official mobile application of ISKCON Hadapsar. We are deeply committed to respecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal data. 1. Information Collection and Processing

We have architected our application based on a "Privacy-First" and "No Forced Login" model. The vast majority of our digital spiritual services (including Live Darshan, Audio Kirtans, and Offline features) are accessible anonymously. We process data only in the following explicit scenarios:

A. Voluntary Financial Transactions (Donations & 80G Exemptions)

To facilitate charitable contributions (Annadaan, Temple Construction, Spiritual Sewa) and process legal tax exemption receipts (80G), we collect:

  • Full Legal Name
  • Contact Information (Mobile Number & Email Address)
  • PAN Card details (Strictly optional, only if the user requests 80G tax exemption)

Financial Security Declaration: We DO NOT store, process, or transmit your credit/debit card numbers, UPI PINs, or banking passwords on our servers. All monetary transactions are securely routed through highly regulated, PCI-DSS compliant third-party payment gateways.

2. Local Device Storage & App State

To deliver a seamless, offline-capable spiritual experience, we utilize your device's local storage (Shared Preferences/SQLite). This data is strictly client-side and is never uploaded to our servers:

  • Offline Japa Mala: Your daily chanting history and counter progress.
  • Interactive Modules: Progress and unlock states for spiritual learning modules and games.
  • UI Preferences: Application settings such as Dark/Light mode toggles and multi-language selections (Hindi, Marathi, English).

3. Device Permissions & Background Services

To execute core application functionalities, we transparently request the following device permissions:

  • Internet & Network State: Essential for fetching dynamic data, live YouTube darshan, ad-serving, and cloud synchronization.
  • Foreground Services (Background Audio): Our application features an advanced media player allowing devotees to listen to Kirtans and Aartis while using other apps or when the screen is locked. We utilize Android Foreground Services (with a persistent notification) strictly to maintain this continuous audio playback without interruption.
  • Location (Map Redirection): We do NOT track your real-time GPS coordinates. The "Navigate Area" feature simply passes destination coordinates to third-party mapping applications installed on your device.

4. External Links, E-Commerce & Promotions

Our ecosystem extends beyond this app. We provide integrated links leading to our official external E-commerce portals (for purchasing verified spiritual books, Puja items, and merchandise). Furthermore, we feature cross-promotional sections highlighting our other official mobile applications.

Please be advised that clicking these links navigates you away from this App. Interactions, purchases, and data collection on those external platforms are governed entirely by their respective Privacy Policies and Terms of Service. We bear no liability for data processed by external sites.

5. Third-Party Analytics & Advertising

We leverage industry-leading third-party infrastructures to ensure global scalability, crash reporting, and content delivery:

  • Google Firebase & Crashlytics: Utilized for real-time database management (image sliders, dynamic menus) and monitoring app stability.
  • Google AdMob (Advertising): To sustain server operations, we display non-intrusive advertisements. AdMob may utilize standard device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to serve contextual or personalized ads based on your Google account preferences.
  • YouTube Data API & Blogger: Embedded APIs used exclusively to stream high-definition Live Darshan and host rich-text spiritual articles within the app interface.

6. Age Requirements & Children's Privacy

Important Audience Declaration: This application is constructed for a General Audience and is strictly targeted toward mature spiritual seekers (Individuals aged 13 and above, or 18+ for monetary transactions).

While the app contains interactive learning modules, stories, and spiritual games, these are designed as educational tools for mature users to understand complex Vedic philosophy. This application is NOT directed at children. We do not knowingly solicit, collect, or process personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian believes a minor has submitted personal data (e.g., via the donation form), please contact us immediately for expedited data expungement.

7. Data Retention & Deletion Mechanism

We retain voluntarily provided data (such as donation logs) solely for the duration mandated by Indian taxation and accounting laws. Because our app does not enforce account creation, there is no digital profile to delete.

Your Right to Erasure (Play Store Mandate): If you wish to request the permanent deletion of your communication logs, donation history, or any other data associated with your email/phone number in our systems, you may submit a formal Data Deletion Request.

Please email om12grt@gmail.com with the subject line "Account/Data Deletion Request". Our technical team will process and confirm the complete removal of your records from our active databases within 15-30 business days.

8. Enterprise Data Security

We implement robust, industry-standard cryptographic protocols (including HTTPS/TLS encryption) to secure data transmission between your device and our cloud servers. Despite our rigorous security architecture, no digital transmission over the public internet is infallibly secure. Users transmit information at their own calculated risk.
